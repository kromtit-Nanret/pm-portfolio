## Projects

### 1. Grida — Escrow Platform & Email Archiving Solution
**Company:** Cue Digital Solution | **Role:** Product Manager Intern | **Sep 2025 – March**

#### Escrow Platform (Fintech)

When my team got assigned to research escrow payments in Nigeria, my first reaction wasn't excitement — it was doubt. Nine escrow platforms already existed and nobody was using them consistently. So instead of trying to prove I was right, I started listening. I talked to vendors losing money to disappearing buyers. I talked to buyers receiving wrong products with no way to recover funds. The trust gap was real, and the existing solutions weren't failing because people didn't know about them — something fundamental was misaligned.

That research became the foundation for a multi-sided marketplace solving trust gaps in Nigerian social commerce.

**What I built:**
- Defined product requirements for 5 user personas: buyers, sellers, logistics agents, freelancers, and marketplace owners
- Created journey maps based on primary market research with vendors and buyers
- Designed dispute resolution framework with evidence-based mediation protecting both sides
- Delivered full PRD with functional requirements, acceptance criteria, and risk mitigation strategies

**Success metrics defined:**
- 80%+ transaction completion rate
- Sub-24-hour payout time
- 500 transactions within first 3 months

### 2. Flashboord — OOH Advertising Marketplace
**Role:** Product Manager | **Jan 2025 – Present** | [www.flashboord.com](http://www.flashboord.com)

Flashboord is live. Not perfect. Not finished. But real enough to learn from.

OOH planning rarely feels slow at first. Maps, calls, rate cards, follow-ups, spreadsheets — it feels familiar. You only notice the time loss when deadlines tighten. What stood out to me wasn't just the 18 hours teams spent gathering availability — it was how normal that friction felt to them. The inefficiency had been accepted as part of the job. That's the systemic problem we're solving.

**What I did:**
- Onboarded 10+ media owners to the platform including New Crystal, Folham, Bowbonish, Cueball communication, Docu2data, motion365 and Breandleep
- Achieved **70% activation rate** (7 of 10 completing full inventory listings)
- Led user testing sessions — watching where users hesitated, counting unnecessary clicks, identifying UX friction before it became churn
- Analyzed campaign performance data and user engagement metrics to drive feature prioritization
- Facilitated sprint planning and backlog management (Jira/Trello)
- Researched regulatory requirements across African markets supporting platform localization

**Shift in how I worked over time:**
> *"The work shifted from building features to understanding behavior. From shipping fast to learning faster. From adding more to removing what doesn't work. I spend more time now in user sessions than in design files."*
